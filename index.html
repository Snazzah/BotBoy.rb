Commands

discard | dis-card

^discard [@user] [-find (username)]

Creates a dis-card.

sdiscard | sdis-card

^sdiscard

Creates a server dis-card.

roblox

^roblox (user (username|id))

Displays ROBLOX information.

drpg

^drpg [@user] [-find (username)]

Creates a DRPG dis-card.

mcskin

cat

meme

^meme templateid|line1|line2

osusig

^osusig (osu username)

Sends a osu signature.

urban

^urban (term)

Reads the most upvoted definition of that term.
